How to make a pizza dough

- by Mubeen

  • Water (Room temp)-3/4 cup
  • Sugar-2 tsp
  • Instant Yeast-1 tsp

    • Cover and rest ot for 10 min

  • Maida(seived)-2 cups
  • Salt-1/2 tsp

    • Pour yeast mixture and mix well until dough formed

  • Oil-2 tbs

    • Rest it for 1-2 hours
